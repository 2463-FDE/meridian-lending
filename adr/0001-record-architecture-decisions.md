# ADR 0001: Record architecture decisions

- **Status:** Accepted
- **Date:** 2023-08-14
- **Author:** Halcyon Software Group

## Context

Meridian needs a lightweight record of the significant decisions made while building
the lending platform, so the in-house team can understand "why" later.

## Decision

We will keep short ADRs in `adr/`, numbered sequentially.

## Consequences

Future maintainers can read the rationale. (In practice Halcyon stopped writing these
after ADR 0003.)
